Our endeavor is to make all Sri Lankans feel that our existence must not be politically or solely economically motivated, but to make Sri Lankanness as the driving force in our lives. To inculcate feelings of togetherness among all Sri Lankans and their children living across the world. Togetherness Bringing together all the Sri Lankans communities within and outside Sri Lanka irrespective of race, religion and nationality.

  • Culture

    Organization of art exhibitions for Sri Lankan children, university students and various artists will be held to show the world the Sri Lankan talent.

  • Contributions

    Introducing a program for Sri Lankans who live in foreign countries and other organizations where they could contribute to help students who face financial difficulties to continue their studies with a proper legal method.

  • Sri Lankan Values

    SL Key magazine offers children who live overseas the ability to get to know the values of Sri Lanka and its culture.

  • SL Key Members

    The SL Key Members are ultimately responsible for the management of the Company's mission and vision strategy to empower and bringing together the Sri Lankan communities as one.
