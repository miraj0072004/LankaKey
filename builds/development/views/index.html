A BETTER WORLD

 The main objective of the Sri Lankan Key Foundation is to inculcate feelings of togetherness among all Sri Lankans and their children living across the world and to instill solidarity irrespective of race ethnicity or religion. Our endeavor is to make all Sri Lankans feel that our existence must not be politically or solely economically motivated, but to make Sri Lankanness as the driving force in our lives.. 

OUR VISION

To enhance peaceful co-existence of all Sri Lankan communities irrespective of all socio-economic, linguistic and cultural diversities.

OUR MISSION

To develop capacities of sons and daughters of Mother Sri Lanka by equipping them with necessary know-how and training enabling them to live in harmony and strength.
